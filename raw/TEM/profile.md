## Business Description

Tempus AI operates as a healthcare technology company in the United States. The company provides:

- **Tempus Platform**: A closed-loop, full-stack, bi-directional integration between clinician's desktop and laboratory diagnostic capabilities, analytics platform, and repository of multimodal data
- **Hub**: A clinical application for physicians and healthcare providers for use in diagnostics as an end-to-end application for NGS tests
- **Lens**: A software application for life sciences and advanced precision research
- **Diagnostics Services**: NGS diagnostics, PCR profiling, and other anatomic and molecular pathology tests
- **Large-Panel Testing**: Solid tumor and hematologic testing through multiple assays (xT and xR), offering large panel DNA, RNA full transcriptome, and incidental germline findings
- **Genetic Tests**: Focused on inherited conditions
- **nP Assay**: Pharmacogenomic testing for psychiatric conditions
- **Insights**: Licenses libraries of linked, de-identified clinical, molecular, and imaging data
- **Organoids**: Tumor-derived biological modeling
- **Trials**: Clinical trial matching services
- **Next**: AI platform
- **Algos**: Algorithm-based diagnostics application

## Key Products and Services

## Market Position

Tempus AI is a leading precision medicine company with:
- One of the world's largest libraries of clinical and molecular data (450+ petabytes)
- Strategic collaborations with AstraZeneca, GlaxoSmithKline, Merck, Recursion Pharmaceuticals, and Pathos AI
- Partnership with Blood Cancer United for pediatric acute myeloid leukemia registry
- Strong position in oncology diagnostics and data analytics

The company was formerly known as Tempus Labs, Inc. and changed its name to Tempus AI, Inc. in January 2023.
