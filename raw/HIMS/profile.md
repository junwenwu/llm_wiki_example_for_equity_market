## Business Description

Hims & Hers Health operates as a consumer-first health and wellness platform that connects consumers to licensed healthcare professionals in the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, Germany, the Republic of Ireland, France, Spain, and internationally.

The company offers:
- A range of curated prescription and non-prescription health and wellness products and services available on its websites and mobile application directly to customers
- Personalized health and wellness products
- Over-the-counter drug and device products, cosmetics, and supplement products primarily focusing on general wellness, skincare, sexual health and wellness, and hair care under the Hims & Hers brand
- Laboratory testing services to measure biomarkers
- Medical consultation and post-consultation support services
- Delivery of laboratory testing results services
- Treatments and products for various chronic conditions related to hormone health, weight loss, dermatology, and mental health

Products are available through retail partnerships, in stores, and online.

## Market Position

Hims & Hers is a leading direct-to-consumer telehealth platform focusing on:
- Men's health (hair loss, ED)
- Women's health (skincare, birth control)
- Weight management (GLP-1 medications)
- Mental health services
- Dermatology

The company has seen significant growth in its GLP-1 weight loss treatment offerings.
