## Business Description

Viavi Solutions provides network test, monitoring, and assurance solutions for telecommunications, cloud, enterprises, first responders, military, aerospace, and critical infrastructures in the Americas, the Asia-Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

The company operates in two segments:
- **Network and Service Enablement (NSE)**: Provides testing, monitoring, assurance, and security solutions that address lab and production environments, network management, service assurance, and AIOps for wireless, wireline, cloud, satellite, public safety, military, and infrastructure networks. Offers instruments, microprobes, and perpetual software licenses
- **Optical Security and Performance Products (OSP)**: Leverages core optical coating technologies and volume manufacturing capability to design, manufacture, and sell technologies for anti-counterfeiting, 3D sensing, government and aerospace, automotive, and industrial markets

## Key Products and Services

## Market Position

Viavi is a leading provider of network test equipment and optical security products. Key market positions include:
- Network test and measurement for telecom and data centers
- Optical coatings for anti-counterfeiting (currency, documents)
- 3D sensing technology for consumer electronics
- Aerospace and defense test solutions

The company has a strategic partnership with QuNu Labs for quantum security and advanced network intelligence technologies.
