# CrowdStrike Q4 and Full Year Fiscal 2026 Financial Results

**Source:** ir.crowdstrike.com/news-releases
**Date:** March 3, 2026
**Period:** Fourth Quarter and Full Year Fiscal 2026 (ended January 31, 2026)

---

## Highlights

- **Surpassed $5 billion ending ARR milestone** - accelerated to 24% YoY growth to reach $5.25 billion
- **Record Q4 net new ARR:** $331 million, up 47% year-over-year
- **Achieved positive GAAP net income** and record non-GAAP net income in the quarter
- **Record operating and free cash flow** for both the quarter and year
- **Falcon Flex:** $1.69 billion in ending ARR, up over 120% year-over-year
- **Full year net new ARR:** First year exceeding $1 billion ($1.01 billion)

## Q4 FY26 Financial Highlights

### Revenue
- **Total revenue:** $1.31 billion, a 23% increase vs $1.06 billion in Q4 FY25
- **Subscription revenue:** $1.24 billion, a 23% increase vs $1.01 billion in Q4 FY25

### Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Grew 24% year-over-year to $5.25 billion as of January 31, 2026
- Net new ARR added in quarter: $330.7 million

### Gross Margin
- **GAAP subscription gross margin:** 79% vs 77% in Q4 FY25
- **Non-GAAP subscription gross margin:** 81% vs 80% in Q4 FY25

### Operating Results
- **GAAP loss from operations:** $6.9 million vs $79.3 million in Q4 FY25
- **Non-GAAP income from operations:** $325.8 million vs $224.8 million in Q4 FY25

### Net Income/Loss
- **GAAP net income attributable to CrowdStrike:** $38.7 million vs loss of $86.3 million in Q4 FY25
- **GAAP net income per share (diluted):** $0.15 vs loss of $0.35 in Q4 FY25
- **Non-GAAP net income attributable to CrowdStrike:** $289.1 million vs $205.3 million in Q4 FY25
- **Non-GAAP net income per share (diluted):** $1.12 vs $0.81 in Q4 FY25

### Cash Flow
- **Net cash from operations:** $497.9 million vs $345.7 million in Q4 FY25
- **Free cash flow:** $376.4 million vs $239.8 million in Q4 FY25
- **Cash and cash equivalents:** $5.23 billion as of January 31, 2026
