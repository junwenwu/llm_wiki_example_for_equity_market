# CrowdStrike Q2 FY26 Earnings Report

**Source:** ir.crowdstrike.com  
**Date:** August 27, 2025  
**Quarter:** Q2 FY26 (ended July 31, 2025)

---

## Headline Results

- **ARR:** $4.66 billion (+20% YoY), net new ARR $221.1 million (record Q2)
- **Total Revenue:** $1.17 billion (+21% YoY)
- **Subscription Revenue:** $1.10 billion (+20% YoY)
- **Non-GAAP Income from Operations:** $255.0 million (record)
- **Non-GAAP EPS:** $0.93 (record)
- **Cash Flow from Operations:** $332.8 million (record Q2)
- **Free Cash Flow:** $283.6 million (record Q2)
- **Cash Position:** $4.97 billion (record)

---

## Executive Commentary

### George Kurtz, Founder and CEO

Record Q2 net new ARR of $221 million, over 1,000 Flex customers, and more than 100 re-flexes highlight CrowdStrike as the leader in cybersecurity consolidation. As AI transforms the enterprise, CrowdStrike enables organizations to confidently embrace their AI future from development to deployment, from cloud to endpoint, and from human to agent."

### Burt Podbere, CFO

Our strong execution and business momentum further bolsters our conviction in continued net new ARR acceleration for the back half of fiscal year 2026."

## Non-GAAP Tax Rate Change

Effective Q2 FY26, CrowdStrike adopted a 21.0% long-term projected non-GAAP tax rate, reduced from the previous rate of 22.5%, in connection with the enactment of the One Big Beautiful Bill Act.
