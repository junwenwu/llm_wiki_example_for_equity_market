# CrowdStrike Q3 Fiscal Year 2026 Financial Results

**Source:** ir.crowdstrike.com/news-releases
**Date:** December 2, 2025
**Period:** Third Quarter Fiscal Year 2026 (ended October 31, 2025)

---

## Highlights

- **Record Q3 net new ARR:** $265 million, growth accelerates to 73% year-over-year
- **Ending ARR:** $4.92 billion, up 23% year-over-year
- **Record cash flow from operations:** $398 million
- **Record Q3 free cash flow:** $296 million
- **Falcon Flex subscription model:** Exceeds $1.35 billion in ending ARR, growing more than 200% year-over-year

---

## Executive Commentary

### George Kurtz, Founder and CEO

"CrowdStrike is the enabler of secure AI transformation with the right architecture, the right products, and the right execution. Q3 was one of our best quarters in company history: we achieved record Q3 net new ARR of $265 million, accelerating to 73% year-over-year growth, and ending ARR of $4.92 billion, which accelerated to 23% growth year-over-year. Our single platform strategy coupled with the Falcon Flex subscription model unlocks consolidation, positioning CrowdStrike as the operating system of cybersecurity. We delivered an acceleration in ending ARR growth across our endpoint business as well as Cloud Security, Next-Gen Identity, and Next-Gen SIEM collectively. Marquee partnerships with AWS, EY, CoreWeave, and Kroll validate CrowdStrike's market leadership, driving our conviction in continued growth."

### Burt Podbere, CFO

"We delivered outstanding third quarter results, exceeding expectations across all guided metrics. Total revenue growth accelerated to 22% year-over-year, and we delivered record cash flow from operations of $398 million and record Q3 free cash flow of $296 million. We are capitalizing on the AI-driven demand environment as customers consolidate on the Falcon platform, driving our pipeline to an all-time high. Given this momentum, we are raising our fiscal year 2026 guidance. Additionally, we have increased our second half fiscal year 2026 net new ARR growth expectations to at least 50% year-over-year and remain confident in delivering 20% net new ARR growth for fiscal year 2027 from our increased fiscal year 2026 expectations."

## Q3 FY26 Financial Highlights

### Revenue
- **Total revenue:** $1.23 billion, a 22% increase vs $1.01 billion in Q3 FY25
- **Subscription revenue:** $1.17 billion, a 21% increase vs $962.7 million in Q3 FY25

### Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Grew 23% year-over-year to $4.92 billion as of October 31, 2025
- Net new ARR added in quarter: $265.3 million

### Gross Margin
- **GAAP subscription gross margin:** 78% (same as Q3 FY25)
- **Non-GAAP subscription gross margin:** 81% vs 80% in Q3 FY25

### Operating Results
- **GAAP loss from operations:** $69.4 million vs $55.7 million in Q3 FY25
- **Non-GAAP income from operations:** Record $264.6 million vs $200.7 million in Q3 FY25

### Net Income/Loss
- **GAAP net loss attributable to CrowdStrike:** $34.0 million vs $16.8 million in Q3 FY25
- **GAAP net loss per share (diluted):** $0.14 vs $0.07 in Q3 FY25
- **Non-GAAP net income attributable to CrowdStrike:** Record $245.4 million vs $190.9 million in Q3 FY25
- **Non-GAAP net income per share (diluted):** Record $0.96 vs $0.76 in Q3 FY25

### Cash Flow
- **Net cash from operations:** Record $397.5 million vs $326.1 million in Q3 FY25
- **Free cash flow:** $295.9 million vs $230.6 million in Q3 FY25
- **Cash and cash equivalents:** $4.80 billion as of October 31, 2025
