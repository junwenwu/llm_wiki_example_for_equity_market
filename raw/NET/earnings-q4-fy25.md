# Cloudflare Q4 2025 and Full Year 2025 Financial Results

**Source:** cloudflare.com/press/press-releases
**Date:** February 10, 2026
**Period:** Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 (ended December 31, 2025)

---

## Highlights

- **Q4 2025 revenue:** $614.5 million, representing a 34% increase year-over-year
- **FY 2025 revenue:** $2,167.9 million, representing a 30% increase year-over-year
- **GAAP loss from operations:** $49.2 million, or 8% of total revenue
- **Non-GAAP income from operations:** $89.6 million, or 15% of total revenue
- **RPO year-over-year growth:** 48%
- **Current RPO year-over-year growth:** 34%

---

## Executive Commentary

### Matthew Prince, Co-founder & CEO

"We had an exceptionally strong end to 2025. In Q4, we closed our largest annual contract value deal ever—averaging $42.5 million per year—and total new ACV grew nearly 50 percent year-over-year, our fastest growth rate since 2021. The shift toward AI and agents represents a fundamental re-platforming of the Internet that's driving demand across Cloudflare's services. If agents are the new users of the web, Cloudflare is the platform they run on and the network they pass through. This creates a virtuous flywheel: more agents drive more code to Cloudflare Workers, which fuels demand for our performance, security, and networking services. We were built for this moment and the rise of the Agentic Internet."

## Q4 2025 Financial Highlights

### Revenue
- **Total revenue:** $614.5 million, +33.6% year-over-year

### Gross Profit
- **GAAP gross profit:** $452.6 million (73.6% gross margin) vs $351.3 million (76.4%) in Q4 2024
- **Non-GAAP gross profit:** $460.2 million (74.9% gross margin) vs $356.8 million (77.6%) in Q4 2024

### Operating Income (Loss)
- **GAAP loss from operations:** $49.2 million (8.0% of revenue) vs $34.7 million (7.5%) in Q4 2024
- **Non-GAAP income from operations:** $89.6 million (14.6% of revenue) vs $67.2 million (14.6%) in Q4 2024

### Net Income (Loss)
- **GAAP net loss:** $12.1 million vs $12.8 million in Q4 2024
- **GAAP net loss per share (basic & diluted):** $0.03 vs $0.04 in Q4 2024
- **Non-GAAP net income:** $106.8 million vs $68.8 million in Q4 2024
- **Non-GAAP net income per share (diluted):** $0.28 vs $0.19 in Q4 2024

### Cash Flow
- **Net cash from operating activities:** $190.4 million vs $127.3 million in Q4 2024
- **Free cash flow:** $99.4 million (16.2% of revenue) vs $47.8 million (10.4%) in Q4 2024
- **Cash, cash equivalents, and available-for-sale securities:** $4,101.3 million as of December 31, 2025

## Full Year 2025 Financial Highlights

### Revenue
- **Total revenue:** $2,167.9 million, +29.8% year-over-year

### Gross Profit
- **GAAP gross profit:** $1,615.4 million (74.5% gross margin) vs $1,290.9 million (77.3%) in FY 2024
- **Non-GAAP gross profit:** $1,643.2 million (75.8% gross margin) vs $1,313.6 million (78.7%) in FY 2024

### Operating Income (Loss)
- **GAAP loss from operations:** $207.2 million (9.6% of revenue) vs $154.8 million (9.3%) in FY 2024
- **Non-GAAP income from operations:** $303.9 million (14.0% of revenue) vs $230.1 million (13.8%) in FY 2024

### Net Income (Loss)
- **GAAP net loss:** $102.3 million vs $78.8 million in FY 2024
- **GAAP net loss per share (basic & diluted):** $0.29 vs $0.23 in FY 2024
- **Non-GAAP net income:** $342.9 million vs $269.0 million in FY 2024
- **Non-GAAP net income per share (diluted):** $0.93 vs $0.75 in FY 2024

### Cash Flow
- **Net cash from operating activities:** $603.1 million vs $380.4 million in FY 2024
- **Free cash flow:** $260.6 million (12.0% of revenue) vs $166.9 million (10.0%) in FY 2024

## About Cloudflare

Cloudflare, Inc. (NYSE: NET) is the leading connectivity cloud company on a mission to help build a better Internet. It empowers organizations to make their employees, applications and networks faster and more secure everywhere, while reducing complexity and cost. Cloudflare's connectivity cloud delivers the most full-featured, unified platform of cloud-native products and developer tools.

Powered by one of the world's largest and most interconnected networks, Cloudflare blocks billions of threats online for its customers every day. It is trusted by millions of organizations – from the largest brands to entrepreneurs and small businesses to nonprofits, humanitarian groups, and governments across the globe.
