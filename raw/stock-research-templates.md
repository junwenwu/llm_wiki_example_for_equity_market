# Stock Research Profiles: Company Templates

Templates for tracking key metrics and earnings for your watchlist stocks.

---

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Copy the relevant template** for each stock you're tracking
2. **Update after each earnings report** with latest data
3. **Track quarter-over-quarter trends** in the metrics table
4. **Note your investment thesis** and track whether it's playing out
