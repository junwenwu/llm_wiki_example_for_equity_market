## Key Highlights

1. **Blowout Quarter** - Revenue beat by $1.15B, largest beat in recent history
2. **Server CPU Strength** - Data center and server chip demand driving growth
3. **Intel Foundry Growth** - Foundry revenue +16% YoY, external customer wins
4. **Process Technology** - 14A node attracting major customers (Tesla, SpaceX)
5. **Agentic AI Beneficiary** - Edge/inference compute demand rising
