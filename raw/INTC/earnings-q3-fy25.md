## Key Highlights

1. **Profitable Quarter** - First GAAP profitable quarter in several periods ($0.90 EPS)
2. **Revenue Growth** - +6.2% QoQ to $13.65B
3. **Gross Margin Expansion** - Gross income $5.22B (38.2% margin)
4. **Equity Investment Gains** - Contributed to profitability
5. **Mobileye Spin** - Partial stake sale boosted results

## Management Commentary

Intel showed strong execution with a return to profitability, driven by improving PC demand and data center strength.
