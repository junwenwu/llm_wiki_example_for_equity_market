# Intel Q2 FY25 Earnings Summary

**Report Date:** July 25, 2025 (After Hours)
**Fiscal Period:** April - June 2025

## Key Highlights

1. **Challenging Quarter** - Revenue +1.5% QoQ but profitability pressured
2. **Restructuring Charges** - $1.57B unusual expense impacted results
3. **Margin Pressure** - Gross income $3.54B (27.5% margin)
4. **Cost Cutting Announced** - 15,000 job cuts planned
5. **Dividend Suspended** - Focus on foundry investments

## Management Commentary

Intel announced significant restructuring to focus resources on process technology leadership and regaining competitiveness.
