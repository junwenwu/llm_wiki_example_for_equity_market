## Business Description

Alphabet Inc. offers various products and platforms in the United States, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, the Asia-Pacific, Canada, and Latin America. It operates through three segments:

**Google Services**: Provides products and services including ads, Android, Chrome, devices, Gmail, Google Drive, Google Maps, Google Photos, Google Play, Search, and YouTube. Also includes sale of apps, in-app purchases, digital content, devices, and YouTube consumer subscription services (YouTube TV, YouTube Music and Premium, NFL Sunday Ticket, Google One).

**Google Cloud**: Offers consumption-based fees and subscriptions for AI solutions including AI infrastructure, Vertex AI platform, and Gemini enterprise. Provides cybersecurity, data and analytics services, Google Workspace (Calendar, Gmail, Docs, Drive, Meet), and other enterprise services.

**Other Bets**: Sells transportation and internet services (Waymo, Verily, etc.).

## Key Products & Services

- **Google Search**: Dominant search engine
- **YouTube**: Video streaming and advertising platform
- **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**: Cloud computing services
- **Android**: Mobile operating system
- **Google Ads**: Digital advertising platform
- **Google Workspace**: Enterprise productivity suite
- **Waymo**: Autonomous vehicles (Other Bets)
- **Gemini**: AI models and services

## Market Position

Alphabet/Google is the world's dominant player in search and digital advertising:

- ~90% global search engine market share
- #2 video streaming platform (YouTube)
- #3 cloud provider (GCP vs AWS, Azure)
- Leading AI research with DeepMind and Gemini models
- Dominant mobile OS (Android) with 70%+ global share
- Pioneer in autonomous vehicles through Waymo
