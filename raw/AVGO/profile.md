## Business Description

Broadcom Inc. designs, develops, and supplies various semiconductor devices and infrastructure software solutions internationally. The company operates in two segments: Semiconductor Solutions and Infrastructure Software.

The Semiconductor Solutions segment offers networking connectivity products including custom silicon solutions, ethernet switching and routing, ethernet NIC controllers, physical layer devices, and fiber optic components; wireless device connectivity including RF semiconductor devices, connectivity solutions, custom touch controllers, and inductive charging ASICs; servers and storage system solutions including PCIe switches, SAS and RAID products, fibre channel products, and HDD and SSD solutions; broadband solutions including set-top box and broadband access; and industrial products.

The Infrastructure Software segment includes a private cloud software portfolio (VMware Cloud Foundation, Edge, vSphere foundation, telco cloud platform, private AI), mainframe software (AIOps, automation, database and data management, DevX and DevOps, cybersecurity and compliance management), cybersecurity solutions (endpoint, network, information, application security, identity and access management), and enterprise software.

- **Wireless Connectivity** - Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and GPS solutions
- **Storage Products** - SAS, RAID, and fibre channel controllers
- **Mainframe Software** - Enterprise software solutions
- **Cybersecurity Solutions** - Symantec enterprise security products

## Market Position

Broadcom is one of the world's leading semiconductor and infrastructure software companies. Following the acquisition of VMware, the company has become a major player in enterprise software and cloud infrastructure. Broadcom is a key supplier of custom AI accelerators for hyperscale data center customers, positioning it as a critical player in the AI infrastructure buildout. The company serves enterprise and data center networking, AI networking and connectivity, home connectivity, broadband access, telecommunications, data center servers and storage, and industrial markets.
