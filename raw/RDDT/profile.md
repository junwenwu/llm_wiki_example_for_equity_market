## Business Description

Reddit, Inc. operates a digital community in the United States and internationally. The company's platform enables users to engage in conversations, explore passions, research new hobbies, exchange goods and services, create new communities and experiences, share content, and find belonging. Reddit organizes communities based on specific interests (called subreddits) that enable users to engage in conversations by sharing experiences, submitting links, uploading images and videos, and replying to one another.

## Key Products and Services

- **Reddit Platform**: Digital community platform with interest-based communities (subreddits)
- **Reddit Premium**: Ad-free subscription service with additional features
- **Reddit Advertising**: Self-serve and managed advertising solutions
- **Reddit Data Licensing**: AI/LLM training data partnerships with technology companies
- **Reddit API**: Developer access to Reddit's platform and data

## Market Position

Reddit is one of the largest social media platforms focused on community-driven content and discussions. The platform serves as a major source of authentic, human-generated content valuable for AI training and search optimization.
