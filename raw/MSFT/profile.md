## Business Description

Microsoft Corporation develops and supports software, services, devices, and solutions worldwide. The company operates through three segments:

**Productivity and Business Processes**: Offers Microsoft 365 commercial, enterprise mobility + security, Windows commercial, Power BI, Exchange, SharePoint, Microsoft Teams, security and compliance, and Copilot. Also includes Microsoft 365 consumer products, LinkedIn, and Dynamics 365 cloud-based applications.

**Intelligent Cloud**: Provides server products and cloud services including Azure, GitHub, Nuance Healthcare, and virtual desktop offerings. Also includes enterprise support services, industry solutions, and Microsoft Partner Network.

**Personal Computing**: Includes Windows OEM licensing, Surface devices and PC accessories, Xbox hardware and gaming services (Game Pass, cloud gaming), and search/news advertising through Bing, Microsoft News, and Edge.

## Key Products & Services

- **Azure**: Cloud computing platform (IaaS, PaaS, AI services)
- **Microsoft 365**: Productivity suite (Office, Teams, Outlook)
- **Windows**: Operating system
- **LinkedIn**: Professional networking platform
- **GitHub**: Developer platform and code repository
- **Xbox**: Gaming hardware and services
- **Dynamics 365**: Business applications (ERP/CRM)
- **Copilot**: AI assistant across products

## Market Position

Microsoft is one of the world's largest technology companies and a leader in enterprise software, cloud computing, and productivity tools:

- #2 cloud provider globally (Azure vs AWS)
- Dominant position in enterprise productivity (Office 365)
- Strong AI strategy with OpenAI partnership and Copilot integration
- Growing gaming business with Xbox and Activision Blizzard acquisition
- Leading developer platform through GitHub
