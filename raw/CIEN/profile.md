## Business Description

Ciena Corporation is a network technology company that provides hardware, software, and services for various network operators in the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, the Asia Pacific, Japan, and India. The company specializes in optical networking, packet networking, and software solutions that enable high-capacity data transport and network automation for service providers, enterprises, and data center operators.

## Key Products and Services

**Networking Platforms Segment:**
- 6500 Packet-Optical Platform: Flexible network infrastructure
- Waveserver: Modular interconnect system for data centers
- 6500 Reconfigurable Line System: Optical networking
- Coherent pluggable transceivers: High-speed optical modules
- 3000/5000 family: Service delivery and aggregation platforms
- 8100 Coherent Routing platforms: Routing solutions

**Platform Software and Services Segment:**
- Navigator Network Control Suite
- Software subscription services
- Consulting, migration, and integration services
- Installation and technical support

**Blue Planet Automation Software Segment:**
- Inventory management and orchestration
- Route optimization and analysis
- Unified assurance and analytics
- Network automation platform

**Global Services Segment:**
- Advisory and enablement services
- Implementation services
- Maintenance, support, and learning services

## Market Position

Ciena is a leading provider of optical and packet networking solutions, positioned as critical infrastructure provider for AI data center build-outs. The company benefits from increasing bandwidth demands driven by AI workloads and cloud computing growth.
