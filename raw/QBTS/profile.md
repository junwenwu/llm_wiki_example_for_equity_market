## Business Description

D-Wave Quantum Inc. engages in the development and delivery of quantum computing systems, software, and services worldwide. The company is a pioneer in quantum annealing technology and provides commercial quantum computing solutions to enterprises. D-Wave operates both annealing quantum computers and is developing gate-model quantum systems, positioning itself as a dual-platform quantum computing company.

## Key Products and Services

- **D-Wave Advantage**: Annealing quantum computing systems with 5,000+ qubits
- **D-Wave Advantage 2**: Next-generation quantum computer with 7,000+ qubits
- **Leap Quantum Cloud Service**: Cloud-based access to quantum computers and hybrid solvers
- **Ocean SDK**: Python-based software development kit for building quantum applications
- **Leap Hybrid Solver Service**: Combination of quantum and classical computation
- **D-Wave Launch**: Phased approach to identify and build production quantum applications
- **Training and Consulting**: Quantum computing education and implementation services

## Market Position

D-Wave is the first commercial quantum computing company and a leader in quantum annealing technology. The company competes with gate-model quantum computing companies like IonQ, Rigetti, and IBM Quantum while pursuing a dual-platform strategy.
