# AppLovin Q4 FY25 Earnings Report

**Source:** Seeking Alpha / Investor Relations  
**Date:** February 11, 2026  
**Quarter:** Q4 2025 (ended December 31, 2025)

---

## Headline Results

- **Revenue:** $1.66 billion (beat estimate by $45.93M)
- **EPS (GAAP):** $3.24 (beat estimate by $0.30)
- **EPS (Normalized):** $3.48 (beat estimate by $0.37)

---

## Company Overview

AppLovin Corporation (NASDAQ: APP) is a mobile technology company that provides marketing software and solutions for advertisers and app developers. The company's primary platform, Axon (formerly AppLovin), powers user acquisition and monetization through AI-driven advertising technology.

---

## Recent Business Highlights

### S&P 500 Inclusion (Q3 2025)
- Company was formally added to the S&P 500
- Reflects enhanced market visibility and institutional focus

### Self-Service Platform (Axon Ads Manager)
- Launched October 1, 2025 on referral-only basis
- Early week-over-week spend growth of ~50%
- Broad set of shopping categories represented
- Quality-controlled onboarding process maintained

### International Expansion
- Opened international traffic for web/shop advertisers (excluding EU)
- English-speaking markets (Canada, Australia, New Zealand) leading expansion
- Localization for Japan and Korea planned as platform scales
- EU represents low teens percentage of business (GDPR build-out deferred)

### Brand Rebrand
- Ad platform rebranded to "Axon"
- Ads Manager launched on referral-only basis
