## Company Overview
AppLovin Corporation is a mobile technology company that offers marketing software and tools for app developers. The company's platform uses machine learning to improve ad targeting and monetization for mobile apps.

Products and Services:
- Axon Ads Manager: AI-powered marketing solutions for developers
- MAX: In-app bidding technology for publishers
- Adjust: Measurement and analytics marketing platform

Growth Outlook:
- Expanding AI capabilities to improve ad efficiency
- Growing connected TV platform through Wurl acquisition
