## Business Description

Coherent Corp. develops, manufactures, and markets engineered materials, optoelectronic components and devices, and laser systems for use in the industrial, communications, electronics, and instrumentation markets worldwide. The company was formerly known as II-VI Incorporated and changed its name to Coherent Corp. in September 2022 following the acquisition of the original Coherent, Inc.

## Key Products and Services

**Networking Segment:**
- Transceivers for datacom and telecom applications
- Optical systems and subsystems
- Semiconductor devices (VCSELs, edge-emitting lasers, pump lasers)
- Coherent pluggable modules (400G, 800G, 1.6T)

**Materials Segment:**
- Engineered materials and laser optics
- Thermoelectric components
- Advanced ceramics and metal-matrix composites
- Silicon carbide (SiC) substrates for power electronics
- Optical coatings and filters

**Lasers Segment:**
- Excimer lasers for semiconductor manufacturing
- Solid-state and CO2 lasers
- Laser systems for industrial applications
- Precision manufacturing and scientific research lasers

## Market Position

Coherent is a leading provider of optical components and lasers critical to AI data center infrastructure. The company's transceivers and optical solutions enable high-speed data transmission between GPUs in AI clusters. Its silicon carbide business serves the growing EV and power electronics markets.
