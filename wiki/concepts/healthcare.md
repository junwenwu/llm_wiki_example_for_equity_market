# Healthcare Technology

Technology companies transforming healthcare delivery through telehealth, AI diagnostics, and precision medicine.

---

## Key Segments

### Telehealth

Direct-to-consumer healthcare platforms providing virtual consultations and treatment delivery.

**Characteristics:**
- Online consultations with licensed providers
- Prescription fulfillment and delivery
- Subscription-based care models
- Focus on chronic conditions (ED, hair loss, mental health, weight loss)

**Key Player:** [[hims]] - leading DTC telehealth platform

### Precision Medicine / AI Diagnostics

Using genomics and AI to personalize treatment decisions.

**Characteristics:**
- Genomic sequencing and analysis
- Clinical data aggregation
- AI-powered diagnostic insights
- Pharma research partnerships

**Key Player:** [[tem]] - AI-enabled precision medicine platform

---

## Market Drivers

1. **Consumer demand for convenience** - Patients want healthcare on their terms
2. **GLP-1 weight loss drugs** - Massive market opportunity (Ozempic, compounded alternatives)
3. **AI in diagnostics** - Faster, more accurate analysis of medical data
4. **Value-based care** - Shift from fee-for-service to outcomes-based reimbursement

---

## Risks

- **Regulatory scrutiny** - FDA oversight of compounded drugs, telehealth regulations
- **Reimbursement challenges** - Insurance coverage for new modalities
- **Data privacy** - HIPAA compliance, patient data security
- **Competition** - Traditional healthcare systems expanding digital offerings
