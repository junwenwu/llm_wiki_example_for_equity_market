# Technical Analysis

Investment analysis method that evaluates securities by analyzing price and volume data, chart patterns, and statistical indicators.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Price discounts everything**: All information is reflected in price
2. **Prices move in trends**: Trends persist until reversal signals
3. **History repeats**: Patterns recur due to market psychology

---

## Chart Types

| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Line** | Closing prices only | Big picture trends |
| **Bar (OHLC)** | Open, high, low, close | Full price data |
| **Candlestick** | Visual OHLC display | Pattern recognition |
| **Point & Figure** | Price movement only | Support/resistance |

See [[candlestick-charts-basics]] for detailed pattern analysis.

---

## Key Concepts

### Support & Resistance
- **Support**: Price level where buying interest emerges
- **Resistance**: Price level where selling pressure appears
- Breaks of these levels signal momentum shifts

### Trends
- **Uptrend**: Higher highs, higher lows
- **Downtrend**: Lower highs, lower lows
- **Sideways**: Range-bound, no clear direction

### Volume
- Confirms price moves (strong volume = valid move)
- Divergence warns of potential reversals

---

## Common Indicators

| Indicator | Type | Purpose |
|-----------|------|---------|
| **Moving Averages** | Trend | Smooth price data, identify direction |
| **RSI** | Momentum | Overbought/oversold conditions |
| **MACD** | Momentum | Trend strength and crossovers |
| **Bollinger Bands** | Volatility | Price envelope around MA |

---

## Pattern Categories

### Reversal Patterns
- Head and shoulders
- Double tops/bottoms
- Morning/evening stars (candlestick)

### Continuation Patterns
- Flags and pennants
- Triangles
- Rectangles
