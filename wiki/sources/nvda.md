## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: AI infrastructure buildout is early innings; dominant CUDA ecosystem creates moat; full-stack provider from chips to software.

**Bear case**: Hyperscalers developing custom silicon; high valuation; potential demand normalization after initial AI capex surge.
