## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: AI data center buildout drives fiber demand; display recovery; Gorilla Glass franchise; diversified end markets; dividend payer.

**Bear case**: Cyclical end markets; display oversupply risk; capital intensive; slower growth than pure-play AI names.
