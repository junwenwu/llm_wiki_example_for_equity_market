# Stock Research Templates

Source summary providing tracking templates for the user's stock watchlist organized by category.

---

## Portfolio Categories

### Tech Giants (MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, TSLA)
- Large-cap, established market positions
- Focus: Cloud growth, AI integration, margin expansion
- Thesis: Platform dominance, recurring revenue

### AI/Data Center Semis (NVDA, AMD, AVGO)
- High-growth AI infrastructure plays
- Focus: Data center revenue, GPU market share
- Thesis: AI CapEx cycle beneficiaries

### Traditional Semis (INTC, MU)
- Cyclical exposure, turnaround stories
- Focus: Process node progress, memory pricing
- Thesis: Valuation recovery, restructuring

### Foundry (TSM)
- Monopoly on advanced manufacturing
- Focus: Utilization, node transitions
- Thesis: Indispensable infrastructure

### Specialty/Optical (LITE, COHR, AXTI)
- Niche semiconductor exposure
- Focus: Datacom growth, AI connectivity
- Thesis: Picks-and-shovels for AI buildout

### Semiconductor ETFs (SMH, SOXX, IGV)
- Diversified sector exposure
- Focus: Holdings, expense ratios
- Thesis: Reduce single-stock risk

---

## Research Template Fields

For each ticker, track:
1. **Company/Ticker**: Name and symbol
2. **Current Price**: Last traded price
3. **52-Week Range**: High/low context
4. **Market Cap**: Size classification
5. **P/E Ratio**: Valuation metric
6. **Key Thesis**: Why own this stock
7. **Key Risks**: What could go wrong
8. **Next Catalyst**: Upcoming event to watch
9. **Target Price**: Personal price target
10. **Position Size**: Current allocation

## Watchlist Status Categories

| Status | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Core** | High-conviction, long-term holds |
| **Active** | Trading positions, shorter timeframes |
| **Watchlist** | Researching, waiting for entry |
| **Avoid** | Thesis broken, no current interest |
