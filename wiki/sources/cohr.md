# Coherent Corp. (COHR)

Engineered materials, optoelectronic components, and laser systems company. Major supplier of optical transceivers for AI data centers.

## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: #1 in AI data center optical transceivers; secular growth from bandwidth demand; vertically integrated from materials to modules.

**Bear case**: Integration of II-VI merger still ongoing; capital intensive; cyclical industrial exposure.
