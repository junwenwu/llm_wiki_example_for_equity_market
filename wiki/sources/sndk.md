## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: NAND cycle recovery; AI data storage demand; pure-play flash company post-spinoff; cost synergies from restructuring.

**Bear case**: Commodity pricing pressure; competition from Samsung, Micron, SK Hynix; capital intensive; cyclical industry.
