# Reddit, Inc. (RDDT)

Community-driven social media platform with interest-based discussion forums (subreddits). Valuable source of human-generated content for AI training.

## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: Unique authentic content moat; AI data licensing is high-margin recurring revenue; advertising still early innings; international expansion.

**Bear case**: Content moderation challenges; user growth deceleration; competition from Discord/Twitter; ad market cyclicality.
