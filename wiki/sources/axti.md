## Key Products

- **Indium Phosphide (InP)**: Data center optical connectivity, 5G, fiber optics, silicon photonics
- **Gallium Arsenide (GaAs)**: Wi-Fi/IoT, LEDs, VCSELs, 3D sensing
- **Germanium**: Satellite solar cells, infrared detectors
- **Raw Materials**: Purified gallium, pBN crucibles

## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: Critical supplier for AI data center optical connectivity (800G/1.6T transceivers); plans to double InP capacity by 2027; vertically integrated.

**Bear case**: Small scale, limited profitability; China operations risk; execution on capacity expansion; high valuation vs fundamentals.
