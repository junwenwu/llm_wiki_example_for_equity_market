# Advanced Micro Devices (AMD)

Fabless semiconductor company competing in CPUs, GPUs, and AI accelerators against Intel and NVIDIA.

---

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Sector** | Information Technology |

| **Employees** | 31,000 |
| **Founded** | 1969 |
| **HQ** | Santa Clara, CA |

## Business Segments

- **Data Center**: EPYC server CPUs, Instinct AI accelerators
- **Client**: Ryzen desktop/laptop CPUs
- **Gaming**: Radeon GPUs, console chips (PS5, Xbox)
- **Embedded**: Xilinx FPGAs, adaptive SoCs

## Key Products

- EPYC server processors
- Instinct MI300 AI accelerators
- Ryzen consumer CPUs
- Radeon graphics
- Xilinx FPGAs (acquired 2022)

## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: Data center share gains vs Intel; MI300 competing for AI workloads; diversified portfolio with Xilinx.

**Bear case**: Distant second to NVDA in AI; software ecosystem less mature than CUDA; intense competition.
