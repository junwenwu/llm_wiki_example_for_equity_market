## Investment Thesis

**Bull case**: Fastest-growing telehealth platform; GLP-1 weight loss opportunity; high gross margins; DTC model scales efficiently.

**Bear case**: Regulatory risk on compounded drugs; GLP-1 supply constraints; customer acquisition costs; competition from traditional healthcare.
