## Business Segments

- **Network Enablement (NE)**: Test and measurement for telecom
- **Service Enablement (SE)**: Network performance monitoring
- **Optical Security & Performance (OSP)**: Anti-counterfeiting, 3D sensing

## Key Products

- Network test equipment (fiber, 5G, wireless)
- Performance management software
- Anti-counterfeiting pigments for currency
- 3D sensing filters for smartphones/AR
